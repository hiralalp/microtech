Quality Control Process

1

Raw Material Inspection

Thorough inspection and testing of all raw materials before production. We verify material composition, certificates, and compliance with specifications.

2

In-Process Quality Checks

Continuous monitoring during manufacturing to ensure dimensional accuracy, surface finish, and adherence to specifications at every stage.

3

Final Product Testing

Comprehensive testing including hydro testing, torque testing, dimensional verification, and visual inspection before dispatch.

4

Documentation & Traceability

Complete documentation including MTC 3.1, RMTC, heat charts, test reports ensuring full traceability of every product.

Testing & Inspection Facilities

Hydro Testing

In-house hydrostatic testing facility to ensure pressure integrity and leak-proof performance of all products.

Torque Testing

Precision torque testing to verify mechanical strength and assembly specifications of fittings and flanges.

Material Testing

Advanced material testing including chemical composition analysis, hardness testing, and metallurgical examination.

Dimensional Inspection

State-of-the-art measuring equipment ensures all products meet exact dimensional specifications.

Visual Inspection

Thorough visual inspection to detect surface defects, finish quality, and overall product appearance.

PMI Testing

Positive Material Identification (PMI) testing to verify material grade and chemical composition.
