About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Quality Flanges & Pipe Fittings

Welcome to Monarch Forge & Fittings

Monarch Forge & Fittings is a leading manufacturer and exporter of high-quality Flanges and Pipe Fittings, serving industries worldwide with excellence and precision. Established with a vision to provide superior quality products, we have grown to become a trusted name in the steel industry.

Our extensive product range includes Flanges and Pipe Fittings manufactured in various premium materials including Stainless Steel, Duplex Steel, Alloy Steel, Carbon Steel, Copper Nickel, SMO 254, Alloy 20, Hastealloy, Inconel, Monel, Nickel, Titanium, and Aluminium.

We pride ourselves on our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities equipped with advanced machinery and technology. Our commitment to quality is reflected in every product we manufacture, ensuring they meet and exceed international standards.
