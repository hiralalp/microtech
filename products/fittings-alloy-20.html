Product Overview

MicroTech Stainless Steel Co. offers premium Alloy 20 (UNS N08020) Pipe Fittings, an austenitic nickel-chromium-molybdenum-copper alloy specifically designed for superior resistance to sulfuric acid and chloride-induced corrosion. Originally developed for sulfuric acid service, Alloy 20 provides exceptional resistance to stress corrosion cracking, pitting, and crevice corrosion in chloride-contaminated environments.

The unique composition featuring 33% nickel, 20% chromium, 2.5% molybdenum, and 3% copper delivers outstanding performance in sulfuric acid concentrations up to 40% at temperatures up to 212Â°F (100Â°C), mixed acids including sulfuric-nitric, phosphoric acid, and various chemical processing environments. The niobium stabilization prevents intergranular corrosion after welding and thermal exposure.

Our Alloy 20 fittings are manufactured to ASME B16.9 and ASTM B366 standards in butt weld configuration including elbows, tees, reducers, caps, and stub ends. Available in sizes from 1/2" to 12", each fitting undergoes comprehensive testing including PMI verification, intergranular corrosion testing (ASTM A262 Practice E), and dimensional inspection to ensure reliable performance in chemical processing, pharmaceutical, petrochemical, and food processing applications.
