Product Overview

Monarch Stainless Steel Co. manufactures and supplies comprehensive range of Stainless Steel Pipe Fittings in various types including butt weld fittings, socket weld fittings, and threaded fittings. Our stainless steel fittings are manufactured from premium quality materials in grades 304/304L, 316/316L, 321, 347, 310, and 904L, ensuring superior corrosion resistance, durability, and performance across diverse industrial applications.

Our product range includes elbows (90°, 45°, 180°), tees, reducers (concentric and eccentric), caps, stub ends, crosses, and lap joint fittings manufactured to international standards including ASME B16.9, ASME B16.11, MSS SP-43, and MSS SP-79. Available in seamless and welded construction with sizes ranging from 1/2" to 48" in various schedules and wall thicknesses.

Each fitting undergoes rigorous quality control including dimensional inspection, PMI testing, hydrostatic testing, and surface finishing to meet customer specifications and international standards. Our stainless steel fittings find extensive applications in chemical processing, petrochemical, pharmaceutical, food processing, oil and gas, power generation, and marine industries.

Key Features & Benefits

Excellent Corrosion Resistance

Superior resistance to corrosion in atmospheric, chemical, and marine environments. SS 316/316L provides enhanced resistance to chlorides and acidic media compared to SS 304/304L.

High-Temperature Performance

Maintains strength and corrosion resistance at elevated temperatures. SS 321 and 347 grades offer excellent resistance to intergranular corrosion in high-temperature applications.

Easy Installation & Welding

Excellent weldability without preheating requirements. Compatible with all standard welding processes including GTAW, GMAW, and SMAW.

Hygienic & Clean

Smooth surface finish ideal for food, pharmaceutical, and biotechnology applications. Non-porous surface prevents bacterial growth and contamination.

High Strength & Durability

Excellent mechanical properties with good strength-to-weight ratio. Long service life reduces maintenance and replacement costs.

Quality Certified

Manufactured and tested to international standards with full material traceability and test certificates including EN 10204 3.1/3.2.
