Stainless Steel Flanges

Premium Quality Stainless Steel Flanges for Critical Applications

Product Overview

MicroTech Stainless Steel Co. manufactures and supplies high-quality Stainless Steel Flanges in various grades and types. Our flanges are manufactured to international standards including ASME B16.5, ASME B16.47, DIN, and BS specifications, ensuring compatibility with global piping systems.

Stainless Steel Flanges offer excellent corrosion resistance, high strength, and durability, making them ideal for applications in chemical processing, oil & gas, petrochemical, pharmaceutical, food processing, and marine industries.

We offer a comprehensive range of stainless steel flanges including Slip-On Flanges, Weld Neck Flanges, Blind Flanges, Socket Weld Flanges, Lap Joint Flanges, Threaded Flanges, and Orifice Flanges in various sizes and pressure ratings.
