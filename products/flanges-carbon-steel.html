Product Overview

MicroTech manufactures Carbon Steel Flanges that provide economical solutions for general purpose piping systems. These flanges offer good mechanical properties and are suitable for a wide range of industrial applications.

Carbon Steel Flanges are widely used in oil & gas, water treatment, construction, and general industrial piping systems where corrosion is not a primary concern or where protective coatings can be applied.

Available in ASTM A105, A350 LF2, and A694 grades, conforming to ASME B16.5 and other international standards.
