Product Overview

Monarch Stainless Steel Co. supplies premium Hastealloy Pipe Fittings in C-276, C-22, B-2, and B-3 grades, engineered for superior corrosion resistance in the most aggressive chemical processing environments. Hastealloy nickel-based superalloys provide exceptional resistance to both oxidizing and reducing acids, stress corrosion cracking, pitting, and crevice corrosion in chloride-contaminated environments.

Hastealloy C-276 and C-22 offer broad-spectrum corrosion resistance from oxidizing to reducing conditions, while B-2 and B-3 grades excel in reducing acid environments including hydrochloric and sulfuric acids. The high nickel, molybdenum, and chromium content combined with low carbon ensures excellent weldability and resistance to grain boundary precipitation.

Our fittings are manufactured to ASME B16.9, ASTM B366, and MSS SP-43 standards in butt weld configuration including elbows, tees, reducers, caps, and stub ends. Available in sizes from 1/2" to 12", each fitting undergoes comprehensive testing including PMI verification, intergranular corrosion testing, and dimensional inspection to ensure reliable performance in chemical processing, pharmaceutical, pollution control, and pulp & paper applications.

Key Features & Benefits

Superior Corrosion Resistance

Exceptional resistance to both oxidizing (ferric chloride, cupric chloride) and reducing (hydrochloric acid, sulfuric acid) conditions. C-276/C-22 offer broad-spectrum resistance.

Chloride Environment Performance

Outstanding resistance to chloride-induced pitting, crevice corrosion, and SCC. High molybdenum/tungsten content provides maximum localized corrosion resistance.

Reducing Acid Excellence

B-2 and B-3 grades specifically optimized for hydrochloric acid and sulfuric acid. Superior performance in non-oxidizing acid environments at all concentrations.

Excellent Weldability

Low carbon content (≤0.01%) ensures excellent weldability without post-weld heat treatment. No grain boundary precipitation concerns with standard welding procedures.

Mixed Acid Performance

Excellent resistance to complex chemical mixtures containing oxidizing and reducing species. Ideal for multi-purpose chemical plants with diverse corrosive media.

NACE Compliant

Available in NACE MR0175/ISO 15156 compliant grades for sour gas service. Full material traceability and corrosion testing per industry specifications.

Industrial Applications

Reactors, distillation columns, heat exchangers handling hydrochloric, sulfuric, phosphoric, acetic acid, and complex chemical mixtures in multi-purpose plants.

Process equipment for pharmaceutical synthesis, API manufacturing, reactors, and high-purity transfer systems requiring corrosion resistance with cleanability.

Flue gas desulfurization systems, scrubbers, waste gas treatment, emission control equipment, and environmental systems handling acidic condensates.

Pulp & Paper

Bleaching equipment, chlorine dioxide generators, digesters, chemical recovery systems, and modern pulp bleaching operations.

Waste Treatment

Hazardous waste incineration, acid recovery, chemical waste neutralization, and metal recovery processes handling mixed aggressive waste streams.
