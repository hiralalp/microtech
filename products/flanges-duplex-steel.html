Product Overview

MicroTech manufactures premium Duplex Steel Flanges that combine the benefits of austenitic and ferritic stainless steels. These flanges offer superior strength, excellent corrosion resistance, and cost-effectiveness for demanding applications.

Duplex Steel Flanges are ideal for applications requiring high strength and resistance to stress corrosion cracking, making them perfect for offshore oil & gas, chemical processing, and marine applications.

Available in grades 2205, 2507, S31803, and S32750, our duplex steel flanges are manufactured to ASME B16.5, ASME B16.47, and other international standards.
